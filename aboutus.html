About RRSC - West, NRSC, ISRO

RRSC-West, Jodhpur is working closely with different stakeholders of natural resources management in the region since last three decades. Besides national priorities, it is also taking care of the requirements of geospatial technologies in the region and the priorities of the state governments. Based on the priority areas, the centre has identified the dominant and secondary regional issues, and pursuing different application projects addressing them in a focused manner. Over the years, the centre has developed expertise accordingly in the region on specific aspects of geospatial technology and applications with a special emphasis on arid ecosystems. Regional issues that needs geospatial attention are being addressed in collaboration with stakeholders, R&D organizations of state/centre and also leading academia in the region. The centre has become a regional knowledge centre and data hub for geospatial applications in the western region of India. In addition, outreach activities towards popularizing the geospatial education especially in Rajasthan state is also another important activity.
